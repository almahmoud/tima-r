You can use this script with the following example:
Rscript src/prepare_dnp.R --input '../lotus-processor/data/processed/210715_dnp_metadata.csv.gz' 

Usage: prepare_dnp.R [--input=<input>] [--output=<output>]

Arguments:
  -i --input=<input>    Where your dnp is located. Works only with special in lab dnp. Cannot be shared
  -o --output=<output>  Output

Options:
  -h --help             Show this screen.
  -V --version          Show version.
